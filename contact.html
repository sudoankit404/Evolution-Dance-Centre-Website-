FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you offer trial classes?

Yes! We offer a free trial class for new students. Contact us to book your trial session and experience our teaching methodology firsthand.

What are the fees for classes?

Fees vary based on the dance form, batch type (online/offline), and package selected. Please contact us for detailed fee structure and available discounts.

Can adults join your classes?

Absolutely! We have specialized batches for adults of all age groups and skill levels. It's never too late to start dancing!

How long does it take to learn?

Learning duration depends on the dance form and your dedication. Basic foundations can be learned in 2-3 months, while mastery takes consistent practice over time.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, we provide certificates upon course completion. Being affiliated with Suro Bharti Sangeet Kala Kendra, our certificates are nationally recognized.

What should I wear for classes?

Comfortable workout clothes and proper dance shoes are recommended. Specific requirements for classical dance forms will be communicated upon enrollment.
